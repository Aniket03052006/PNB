The 5-Phase CBOM Pipeline

Every scan runs through a deterministic five-phase pipeline grounded in NIST FIPS 203, 204, and 205.

01

Asset Discovery

Discovers domains, subdomains, IPs, SSL certificates, and software across your bank's attack surface.

02

Tri-Mode TLS Probing

Three simultaneous probes simulate PQC-capable, classical TLS 1.3, and legacy TLS 1.2 clients.

03

PQC Classification

Q-Score engine evaluates six dimensions: TLS version, key exchange, certificates, cipher strength, agility, and negotiation policy.

04

CBOM Generation

CycloneDX 1.7 Cryptographic Bill of Materials with Ed25519-signed attestation for every scanned asset.

05

Certification Labeling

Issues Elite-PQC, Standard, or Legacy labels. Revocable registry with public verification endpoint.
